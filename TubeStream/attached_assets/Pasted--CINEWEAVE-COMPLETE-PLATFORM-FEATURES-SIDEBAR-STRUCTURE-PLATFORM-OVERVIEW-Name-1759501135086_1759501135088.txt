## **CORE FEATURES**

### **1. CONTENT CONSUMPTION**
- Video player with quality controls (360p to 4K)
- Like/Dislike/Share/Save buttons
- Comments with replies and reactions
- Playlists creation and management
- Watch History and Watch Later
- Notifications system

### **2. CONTENT DISCOVERY**
- **Home Feed** (Algorithmic + Personal Mode)
- **Shorts** (Short-form vertical videos)
- **Trending** (Platform-wide popular content)
- **Explore Sections** (Shopping, Music, Movies, Gaming, etc.)
- **Search** with filters and suggestions

### **3. CHANNEL & CREATOR FEATURES**
- Channel creation and customization
- Subscription system
- Video upload and management
- Analytics dashboard
- Community posts and polls
- Live streaming capability

### **4. UNIQUE PERSONALIZATION FEATURES**

#### **A. PERSONAL MODE**
- Toggle switch in sidebar
- Shows ONLY content from subscribed channels
- No algorithmic recommendations
- Chronological feed from subscriptions

#### **B. PERMANENT CHANNEL BLOCKING**
- Block any channel permanently
- Blocked channels never appear in recommendations
- Block management in settings
- Import/export block lists

#### **C. SPACES SYSTEM**
- Create custom content groups (Gaming Space, Tech Space, etc.)
- Add channels to different spaces
- Space-specific video feeds
- Cross-channel recommendations within spaces
- Collaborative spaces (multiple users)

### **5. USER MANAGEMENT**
- User profiles and channels
- Subscription management
- Watch history controls
- Privacy settings
- Notification preferences

### **6. CONTENT CATEGORIES**
- **Shopping** - Product reviews, hauls, unboxing
- **Music** - Songs, music videos, covers
- **Movies** - Trailers, reviews, analysis
- **Live** - Live streams, events
- **Gaming** - Gameplay, reviews, esports
- **News** - Current events, journalism
- **Sports** - Highlights, analysis, live sports
- **Courses** - Educational content, tutorials
- **Fashion & Beauty** - Makeup, fashion, lifestyle
- **Podcasts** - Audio and video podcasts
